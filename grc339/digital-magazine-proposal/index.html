Introduction

Feastivities

As a food and festival culture magazine, Feastivities is meant for readers who enjoy discovering new food destinations and live music events. This magazine will feature hidden gems and less popular food locations for individuals who like more low profile spots, as well as highlight underrated music artists. Feastivities would have different editions for different popular cities in America and be distributed to their locals and other city dwellers who want to discover more about urban culture.
